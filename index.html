Kristin Olson

Drums + Percussion

KRISTIN

OLSON

is a drummer, percussionist, and educator specializing in pop and Latin drums and percussion. She performs and records with several local groups in the Bloomington and Indianapolis areas, and has shared the stage with artists including Michael Spiro, Wayne Wallace, Ignacio Berroa, Victor Rendon, Valerie Naranjo, Nani Agbeli, So Percussion, and more.


Kristin has taught percussion as a Visiting Lecturer in Music at both Indiana University and Lawrence University. Additional teaching experience includes high school drumline and percussion ensembles, senior drum classes, and private instruction for students ranging from 8 years old to adult.


Moving to Los Angeles in June 2018, Kristin is eager to contribute to popular, Latin, and classical music in the LA creative scene and educational settings.
